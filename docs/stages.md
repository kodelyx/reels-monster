# 🎬 Reels-Monster: Short Guide

Reels-Monster pipeline me total **12 stages (00 to 11)** hain. Har stage ka direct kaam ye hai:

### ✍️ Likhna (Planning)
* **Stage 00 (Topic):** Topic decide karna.
* **Stage 01 (Research):** Facts aur information nikalna.
* **Stage 02 (Script):** Dialogue / voiceover likhna (Hindi me).
* **Stage 03 (Scenes):** Background videos ke ideas/descriptions likhna.
* **Stage 04 (Music Info):** Background music ka style decide karna.

---

### 🎨 Banana (Media Assets)
* **Stage 05 (Avatar):** Bolne wale character (avatar) ki video banana.
* **Stage 06 (Process):** Faltu silent part kaatna, volume normalize karna, aur captions align karna.
* **Stage 07 (Popups):** Text cards design karna aur best Sound Effects match karna.
* **Stage 08 (B-roll):** Background video clips generate karke download karna.
* **Stage 09 (Music):** Gemini se custom music banwana aur save karna.

---

### 🖥️ Merge (Video Editing)
* **Stage 10 (Render):** Sab assets ko merge karke final video render karna.
* **Stage 11 (Trim):** Video borders aur frames ke according final tight trim check.
