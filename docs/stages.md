# 🎬 Reels-Monster: Simple Step-by-Step Guide

Reels-Monster ek **Auto-Reel Editor** hai. Ye 3 aasan steps me kaam karta hai:
1. **Likhna (Text Phase - Stage 00 to 04):** Pehle poora text and planning ready hota hai.
2. **Bananan (Media Phase - Stage 05 to 09):** Phir saare video clips aur music alag-alag generate hote hain.
3. **Merge karna (Editing Phase - Stage 10 & 11):** Aakhir me sab kuch ek saath chipka kar final video ban jata hai.

---

### Step 1: LIKHNA (Planning & Script)

* **Stage 00 (Topic):** Hum concept select karte hain (e.g., *"AI kaise jobs badal raha hai"*).
* **Stage 01 (Preproduction):** AI topic par research karke real facts & figures nikalta hai.
* **Stage 02 (Script):** AI narration likhta hai (Devanagari Hindi me) jo final video me boli jayegi.
* **Stage 03 (Scenes):** AI decide karta hai ki background me kaun se videos chalenge (prompts banti hain).
* **Stage 04 (Music Prompt):** AI music ka style/mood likh kar deta hai (e.g., *"fast electronic beat"*).

---

### Step 2: BANANA (Generating Assets)

* **Stage 05 (Avatar):** AI voice aur narrator character video (bolta hua avatar) generate karta hai.
* **Stage 06 (Process):** Speaks portion ko auto-trim kiya jata hai aur voice volume ko standard normalize karte hain.
* **Stage 07 (Popups):** Narration ke words ke hisab se text popups (visual cards) aur appropriate Sound Effects (Ding, Swoosh) sync kiye jate hain.
* **Stage 08 (B-roll):** Flow API background video clips generate karke download karta hai.
* **Stage 09 (Music):** Gemini Lyria humare music prompt ke hisab se custom background music compose karta hai.

---

### Step 3: MERGE KARNA (Final Video Editing)

* **Stage 10 (Render):** Remotion framework voice, background videos, music, aur popups ko automatic merge karke final video output compile kar deta hai.
* **Stage 11 (Final Trim):** Extra silent video parts ko clean trim kiya jata hai taaki transitions perfectly clean fit ho sakein.
